Finding 6 — Margin
Discounts compress margin by 5.86 percentage points
Non-discounted orders run at 54.73% gross margin. Discounted orders drop to 48.87%. The discount program costs SwiftCart nearly 6 points of margin on more than half its revenue.

Discounts are paying a margin cost for a retention benefit that does not exist in the data. Return rates between discounted and non-discounted orders are virtually identical — 13.41% vs 13.51%.
